Message from Principal

I am very much delighted to introduce Khulna University of Engineering & Technology (KUET), which is one of the leading Higher Education Institutes (HEIs) of Bangladesh, established in 1967 as Khulna Engineering College in a greenery landscape of 101 acres land situated about 12km North from the city Centre.
The university is aimed to take the leadership in promoting technological development and management of the nation by strengthening engineering, science & technological education and research. For decades, KUET has been at the forefront of education, research and nation-building, producing the best graduates in the country. Research and academic activities have also been extended for meaningful collaboration with various Higher Education & Research Institutes from the Asian, European, American and Australian countries. As the Vice-Chancellor of KUET, I am honoured to be at the helm of what is considered as one of the foremost universities in Bangladesh, and I hope to lead through an outstanding period of progress and growth in quality education, research and overall teaching & learning environment of the campus.
I do look forward to welcoming you at KUET to experience the unique learning & sociable environment.
